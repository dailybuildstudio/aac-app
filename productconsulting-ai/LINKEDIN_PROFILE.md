## 6. One thing to NOT do

Don't update your headline + about + featured + experience **all at once**. LinkedIn will broadcast big profile changes to your network (and to Floor & Decor's recruiting team), and a wholesale rewrite can read as "I'm looking."

**Suggested rollout over 3 weeks:**
- **Week 1:** Update Featured (add productconsulting.ai). Low-key — no broadcast.
- **Week 2:** Update About section.
- **Week 3:** Update Headline + Skills order.

If your network notices, it looks like organic evolution. If you slam all three on Tuesday, it looks like you're announcing something.
