# Master Services Agreement (MSA) — Template

> ⚠️ **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** This is a working draft to send to a lawyer for a one-pass review. Don't sign anything based on this template without that review. Laws vary by jurisdiction; your business entity, state of formation, and specific deal structure will all affect what should change. A 1–2 hour review with a small-business attorney is usually $200–500 and a one-time cost that protects every future engagement.

---

**This Master Services Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** ("Effective Date") between:

- **Consultant:** [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] ("Consultant"), a [STATE] [LLC / sole proprietorship / corporation], with its principal place of business at [ADDRESS]. Doing business as **productconsulting.ai**.
- **Client:** [CLIENT LEGAL NAME] ("Client"), a [STATE/COUNTRY] [ENTITY TYPE], with its principal place of business at [CLIENT ADDRESS].

Each is a "**Party**"; together, the "**Parties**."

---

## 1. Services

1.1 Consultant will perform services ("**Services**") for Client as described in one or more **Statements of Work** ("**SOW**") issued under this Agreement. Each SOW is incorporated into this Agreement when signed by both Parties.

1.2 If anything in a SOW conflicts with this Agreement, the SOW governs **only** for the engagement described in that SOW.

1.3 Consultant performs Services as an independent contractor (see Section 11). Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship.

## 2. Fees, Invoices, and Payment

2.1 Fees, payment schedule, and currency are stated in each SOW.

2.2 Unless otherwise stated in a SOW, **50% of the engagement fee is due as a deposit before work begins**; the remaining 50% is due upon delivery of the final deliverable.

2.3 Invoices are due **net-15 days** from receipt. Past-due amounts accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower).

2.4 Consultant may **pause or terminate Services** if any invoice is more than 15 days past due, with 5 business days' written notice.

2.5 All fees are in USD unless a SOW states otherwise. Client is responsible for any applicable sales/use taxes (Consultant is not collecting taxes on Client's behalf unless required by law).

## 3. Term and Termination

3.1 This Agreement starts on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

3.2 **Either Party may terminate** this Agreement for any reason with **30 days' written notice**. Any active SOW continues to completion unless both Parties agree to end it early.

3.3 **Either Party may terminate immediately** if the other Party materially breaches the Agreement and fails to cure the breach within **15 days** of written notice.

3.4 Upon termination, Client pays Consultant for all Services performed through the termination date plus any non-cancellable expenses incurred. Deposits already paid for work not yet delivered are non-refundable except in cases of Consultant material breach.

## 4. Intellectual Property

4.1 **Client Deliverables.** Upon **full payment** of all fees under the relevant SOW, Consultant assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the specific written **Deliverables** identified in that SOW (the audit report, sprint plan, etc.).

4.2 **Consultant's Pre-Existing IP and Methods.** Consultant retains all rights in its frameworks, scoring models, templates, methodologies, and general know-how that exist before the engagement or are developed independently (collectively, "**Consultant IP**"). Consultant grants Client a **perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license** to use Consultant IP solely as embedded in the Deliverables for Client's internal business purposes.

4.3 **Anonymized Learnings.** Consultant may retain and reuse anonymized, aggregated learnings (lessons, patterns, statistics) from the engagement for Consultant's own knowledge base, publications, and future client work — provided no Confidential Information of Client is disclosed.

4.4 **Third-Party IP.** Each Party warrants it has the rights to share with the other any third-party materials it provides. Neither Party warrants the legality of using third-party AI model outputs for any particular purpose.

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1 "**Confidential Information**" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party to the other, marked or reasonably identifiable as confidential, including business plans, product data, customer lists, financials, technical details, and personal data.

5.2 Each Party will (a) use the other's Confidential Information **only to perform the Agreement**; (b) protect it with at least the same care it uses for its own confidential information (and never less than reasonable care); and (c) not disclose it to third parties except to its own employees, contractors, and advisors who need to know and are bound by similar confidentiality obligations.

5.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination of the Agreement for **3 years**, or indefinitely for trade secrets.

5.4 Exceptions: information that is (a) public through no fault of the receiving Party, (b) already known prior to disclosure, (c) independently developed without use of the other's Confidential Information, or (d) required to be disclosed by law (with prompt notice to the disclosing Party where legally permitted).

## 6. Warranties and Disclaimers

6.1 Each Party warrants it has the authority to enter this Agreement.

6.2 Consultant warrants that Services will be performed in a **professional and workmanlike manner** consistent with industry standards.

6.3 **Consultant makes no other warranties — express or implied — including any warranty of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, or that any AI feature recommended will achieve any specific business outcome.** Recommendations are based on professional judgment and the information Client provides; Client retains all decision-making authority for what it builds, ships, and operates.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

7.1 **Cap on liability.** Each Party's total cumulative liability under this Agreement, for any cause, is limited to **the fees paid by Client to Consultant under the relevant SOW in the 12 months preceding the claim**.

7.2 **Exclusion of indirect damages.** Neither Party is liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages — including lost profits, lost revenue, lost data, or business interruption — even if advised of the possibility.

7.3 **Carve-outs.** The limits in 7.1 and 7.2 do not apply to (a) breach of confidentiality, (b) infringement of the other Party's intellectual property, (c) gross negligence or willful misconduct, or (d) indemnification obligations.

## 8. Indemnification

8.1 **Client indemnifies Consultant** against any third-party claim arising from (a) Client's use of any Deliverable in a manner not contemplated by the SOW, (b) Client's products, services, or AI features built using the Deliverables, or (c) Client's negligence or willful misconduct.

8.2 **Consultant indemnifies Client** against any third-party claim that the Deliverables, as delivered, infringe a U.S. patent or copyright — provided Client gives prompt notice and Consultant controls the defense. Consultant has no obligation for claims arising from Client's modifications, combinations with other materials, or use outside the SOW.

## 9. Insurance

Consultant maintains, where reasonably available and proportionate to engagement size, **Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions) insurance**. Certificate available on request.

## 10. Non-Solicitation

For **12 months** after termination of this Agreement, neither Party will directly solicit or hire the other Party's employees or contractors with whom they had direct contact during the engagement, without the other Party's written consent. Generic public job postings are not considered solicitation.

## 11. Independent Contractor

Consultant is an independent contractor, not an employee, partner, or agent of Client. Consultant has sole discretion over how, when, and where Services are performed, subject to deadlines in SOWs. Consultant is responsible for its own taxes, benefits, insurance, and equipment.

## 12. Conflicts of Interest

12.1 Consultant may work with multiple clients in the same industry. Where Consultant is engaged by a direct competitor of Client (defined as a company offering substantially the same product to substantially the same customers), Consultant will disclose this to Client and the Parties will discuss in good faith whether to continue or scope the engagement to avoid conflict.

12.2 Consultant will not use Client's Confidential Information for the benefit of any other client.

## 13. Force Majeure

Neither Party is liable for delays or failures caused by events outside its reasonable control — including natural disasters, pandemic, war, terrorism, government action, internet/cloud outages, or third-party service failures — provided the affected Party gives prompt notice and resumes performance as soon as reasonably possible.

## 14. Notices

All notices must be in writing and sent to the email addresses below (or any updated email address provided in writing):

- **Consultant:** hello@productconsulting.ai
- **Client:** [CLIENT EMAIL]

Email notices are deemed received on the next business day.

## 15. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

15.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of the **State of [STATE]**, USA, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles.

15.2 The Parties will attempt in good faith to resolve any dispute informally within 30 days before pursuing other remedies. Unresolved disputes will be brought in the state or federal courts located in **[COUNTY], [STATE]**, and both Parties consent to that jurisdiction.

15.3 The prevailing Party in any litigation is entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.

## 16. Miscellaneous

- **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement plus any signed SOWs is the complete agreement between the Parties; it replaces any prior agreement or understanding on the subject.
- **Amendments.** Any change must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
- **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent, except to a successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.
- **Severability.** If any provision is unenforceable, the rest of the Agreement remains in effect.
- **Waiver.** A failure to enforce any right is not a waiver of future enforcement.
- **Counterparts and Electronic Signature.** This Agreement may be signed in counterparts, including electronically. Electronic signatures have the same effect as ink signatures.
