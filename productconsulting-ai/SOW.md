## SOW Template 1 — AI Product Audit ($3,000 flat)

**Statement of Work #[SOW-001]**
**Issued under the Master Services Agreement dated [MSA DATE]**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Client** | [CLIENT NAME] |
| **Engagement Title** | AI Product Audit |
| **Start Date** | [START DATE] |
| **End Date** | [START DATE + 14 days] |
| **Fee** | **$3,000 USD, flat** |
| **Payment Schedule** | $1,500 deposit on signature; $1,500 on delivery of Final Audit Report |

### Scope of Services

Consultant will:

1. Review Client's existing product, documentation, and metrics available to Consultant.
2. Conduct **up to 3 stakeholder interviews** (60 min each) with people designated by Client.
3. Identify **5–9 AI opportunities** for Client's product.
4. Score each opportunity using the **5-Dimension Scoring Framework** (User Value, Business Impact, Feasibility, Time to Value, Defensibility).
5. Deliver a **written Final Audit Report** containing:
   - Executive summary
   - Opportunity inventory with scores and rationale
   - A single ranked "build-first" recommendation with implementation guidance
   - A "what NOT to build" section with reasoning
   - A 3-horizon roadmap (now / next / later)
6. Provide a **45-minute walkthrough call** of the Final Audit Report with Client's team.

### What is OUT of Scope
- Writing production code or building features
- Hiring, vendor selection, or procurement
- Data engineering or pipeline work
- Post-delivery support beyond the walkthrough call (separate engagement)
- Any work beyond the items listed above; additional work requires a new SOW

### Client Responsibilities
- Provide Consultant with product access (read-only login or guided walkthrough), relevant analytics, and any internal documents needed to complete the audit
- Designate a primary point of contact who responds to Consultant communications within 2 business days
- Schedule the up-to-3 stakeholder interviews within the first 7 calendar days of the engagement
- Provide feedback on the draft Final Audit Report within 3 business days of receipt

### Timeline / Milestones

| Day | Milestone |
|----|-----------|
| Day 0 | SOW signed, 50% deposit paid, kickoff scheduled |
| Day 1–3 | Kickoff call, product walkthrough, materials shared |
| Day 4–8 | Stakeholder interviews + Consultant analysis |
| Day 9–11 | Draft Final Audit Report delivered for Client review |
| Day 12–13 | Revisions based on Client feedback |
| Day 14 | Final Audit Report + walkthrough call; 50% balance invoiced |

### Acceptance
Final Audit Report is deemed accepted when Client confirms in writing OR 5 business days after delivery if no written objection is received.

## SOW Template 2 — AI Product Sprint ($12,000–$18,000)

**Statement of Work #[SOW-XXX]**
**Issued under the Master Services Agreement dated [MSA DATE]**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Client** | [CLIENT NAME] |
| **Engagement Title** | AI Product Sprint — [FEATURE NAME] |
| **Start Date** | [START DATE] |
| **End Date** | [START DATE + 4–6 weeks, fixed] |
| **Fee** | **$[12,000 / 15,000 / 18,000] USD, flat** *(set based on scope and team complexity)* |
| **Payment Schedule** | 50% deposit on signature; 50% on delivery of Sprint Plan |

### Scope of Services

Consultant will define, scope, and de-risk **one AI feature**: [FEATURE NAME]. Specifically:

1. Up to **6 working sessions** (60–90 min each) with Client's product/engineering team
2. **User research synthesis** — review existing user research or conduct lightweight discovery (up to 5 user interviews)
3. **Feature definition** — JTBD, scope, success metrics, acceptance criteria
4. **Technical de-risking** — review proposed architecture, surface key build/buy decisions, identify edge cases
5. **Prototype or detailed spec** — Consultant will produce **[a working prototype / a detailed feature specification / both]** — see "Deliverables" below
6. **Risk register** — known unknowns and recommended mitigation
7. **Handoff document and walkthrough** — Client's team is set up to build

### Deliverables
- Feature definition document
- One of: working prototype, detailed PRD, or both (per Client preference at kickoff)
- Risk register and recommended go/no-go criteria
- 60-minute handoff walkthrough

### Out of Scope
- Production engineering / shipping the feature itself
- Post-handoff support (covered separately via retainer or fixed-fee sprint extension)
- Any feature other than the one named above

### Client Responsibilities
- Make the Client team available for the scheduled working sessions
- Provide access to existing user research, analytics, code where relevant
- Decide on any open scope questions within 2 business days

### Timeline / Milestones

| Week | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | Kickoff, feature scoping, initial research |
| 2–3 | Working sessions, prototype/spec development |
| 4 | Risk register, draft deliverables to Client |
| 5–6 | Revisions, final deliverables, handoff walkthrough |

### Acceptance
Sprint deliverables are deemed accepted on completion of the handoff walkthrough OR 5 business days after delivery if no written objection is received.

**Statement of Work #[SOW-XXX]**
**Issued under the Master Services Agreement dated [MSA DATE]**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Client** | [CLIENT NAME] |
| **Engagement Title** | Advisory Retainer |
| **Start Date** | [START DATE] |
| **Term** | Month-to-month; either Party may end with 30 days' written notice |
| **Fee** | **$[4,000 / 6,000 / 8,000] USD per month** |
| **Payment Schedule** | Invoiced monthly **in advance**; net-15 |

### Scope of Services — What's Included Each Month

- **2 standing strategy calls** (60 min each), scheduled at Client's preference (typically every other Tuesday)
- **Up to 8 hours of asynchronous review** of Client materials (roadmaps, PRDs, design reviews, AI feature briefs)
- **Slack / email response within 1 business day** during US business hours
- **One written deep dive per month** on a topic of Client's choosing (e.g., a specific feature review, a strategic question, a competitive analysis)
- **Quarterly roadmap review** — a deeper 90-min session every 3 months

### What's Out of Scope (Requires Separate SOW)
- Hands-on prototype or product build work
- Recruiting or hiring decisions for Client roles
- Anything requiring more than 8 hours/month of asynchronous time

### Client Responsibilities
- Designate a primary point of contact
- Share materials at least 24 hours before any review

### Acceptance
Monthly services are deemed delivered at end of each calendar month.

## Internal Operating Notes
- Always issue a **fresh SOW per engagement**, even with returning clients — don't extend old ones.
- The MSA governs the relationship; the SOW governs each piece of work.
- Save executed copies in a dedicated folder (Google Drive: `/productconsulting.ai/clients/[CLIENT]/`).
- Send invoices via Stripe or your invoicing tool — set Net-15 by default.
