## What NOT to put in this email
- ❌ A massive "what to expect" PDF attachment — they'll find it intimidating and skip it. Save the detail for the kickoff doc you send separately.
- ❌ A pricing-mention or upsell — they just paid you; the moment is to deliver, not pitch.
- ❌ "Looking forward to partnering with you" type filler — junior energy.
- ❌ Anything that says "feel free to reach out" — vague invitations are worse than no invitation. Just say "DM or email."

## Bonus — what to do behind the scenes the same hour
1. Send the **kickoff doc** (see `KICKOFF_DOC.md`) as a separate email or shared doc within 24 hours.
2. Set up their client folder.
3. Block the 2-week window in your calendar with a buffer.
4. **Forward yourself a copy** of the signed SOW + the deposit confirmation — easier to audit later.
5. (Optional but powerful) **Voice-memo yourself** what you actually know about this client and what you're nervous about. Future-you on day 7 will be glad you did.
