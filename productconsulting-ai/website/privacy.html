Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Plain-English version: productconsulting.ai is a one-person consulting practice. The only personal information collected on this site is what you voluntarily give me — your name, email, and any context you share — when you book a call or download the framework. I use it to respond to you. I don't sell it, rent it, or use it for anything you didn't ask for.

1. Who runs this site

This site is operated by Amanda Shelton ("I," "me," or "the practice"), trading as productconsulting.ai. Contact: hello@productconsulting.ai.

2. What I collect

I collect only what you give me directly:

  • Name, email, and phone number when you book a teardown call via the scheduling link.
  • Name, email, company, and the questions you answer on the booking form.
  • Name and email when you download the AI Product Audit Scoring Framework.
  • Anything you choose to email me at hello@productconsulting.ai.

I do not currently collect cookies, run behavioral tracking, or use third-party advertising pixels on this site. If that changes (for example, if I add privacy-friendly analytics like Plausible), I'll update this policy and disclose it here.

3. How I use it

  • To respond to your inquiry, run your teardown call, or send you the materials you asked for.
  • To deliver any paid engagement you hire me for (audit, sprint, retainer, fractional).
  • To send you the occasional follow-up email only if you've explicitly opted in (e.g., subscribing to the framework download or a future newsletter).
  • To meet legal, accounting, and tax obligations.

I do not sell, rent, or trade your information. I don't target ads to you based on the data you give me here.

  • Booking and lead-form data: kept as long as we have an active relationship or for up to 24 months after our last contact, whichever is later — then deleted.

  • Email correspondence: kept in my inbox unless you ask me to delete it.

7. Your rights

Wherever you live, you can email hello@productconsulting.ai and ask me to:

  • Tell you what data I have on you.
  • Correct anything that's wrong.
  • Delete it.
  • Unsubscribe from any list you opted into.

If you're in the EU/UK, you have rights under the GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection). If you're in California, you have rights under the CCPA/CPRA (know, delete, correct, opt-out of sale — though I don't sell). I'll honor either set of rights for anyone who asks, regardless of where they live.

I'm based in the United States. The vendors listed above may store data in the U.S. or other countries. If you submit information from outside the U.S., you consent to it being processed in the U.S. and other jurisdictions where these vendors operate.

9. Children's privacy

This site is intended for businesses and professionals. It is not directed at children under 16, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has submitted information, email me and I'll delete it.

10. Security

The site is served over HTTPS. The third-party services I use (Cloudflare, Cal.com, Google) maintain industry-standard security practices. No system is perfectly secure — but I keep the surface area small on purpose: no database I run, no logins, no payment processing on the site itself.

11. Changes to this policy

If I make material changes, I'll update the "last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify anyone who has actively opted in to email contact. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: hello@productconsulting.ai.
