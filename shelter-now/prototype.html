About

Find Shelter Now is a free, anonymous, mobile-first directory of Florida shelters. We do not collect any personal information. We do not require accounts.

During state-declared emergencies, we show real-time hurricane shelter status pulled from FloridaDisaster.org. Year-round, we publish a curated directory of homeless shelters.

What we don't do: domestic violence shelter listings (call 1-800-799-7233), real-time bed counts for homeless shelters (call 211), or anything outside Florida.

Always call 211 to confirm any shelter information before you travel.
